# Foodship: Sci-Fi Food Truckin'
Inspired by the Chinese noodle seller from The Fifth Element. 3D navigation through an urban rainforest of towering megastructures to feed the hungry citizens within.
Some elements of HardWar.

## Setting
Ecumenopolis, cyberpunk rat's nest of skyscrapers, factories, sprawls and slums.

## Mechanics
Track customer activity all over the city with listening probes, get to the customer rushes before the competition.
Hack transmissions and other infrastructure to inject adverts for your eatery.
'Self defend' yourself from rival restauranteurs, hijackers, and megacorp thugs.
Run delivery, catering, or on site service, or poach gigs from others.

## Ship Equipment
Upgrade with tech like homing delivery pods, signal jammers, hacking modules, etc.

## Kitchen Equipment
Oven, microwave, rehydrator, specialized equipment, packager, storage
